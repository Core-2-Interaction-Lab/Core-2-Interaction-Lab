So, I got rid most of the css styles, and left most of the tables and grids on the html. Leaving most of the images, buttons, and other functions that should be in the html file.

The webpage should have a seperate folder, perhaps assets, where they have put their images and buttons in there. Along with a stylesheet file, where they can go back to re-edit their css and website design. They should also properly add their grids and flexbox in their css file, if they were to create one.

Overall, they need to work on their homepage, and their links, or they're overall presentation. Possibly by adding more navigation functions, insteaad of having all the links in one page or the homepage. Some of the text, and typefaces used were questionable, along with their background image.
