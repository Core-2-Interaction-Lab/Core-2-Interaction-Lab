Angel Yu

Souvenirs Around the World

This is a collection made by Angel Yu where she showcasing her father's remarkable collection of souvenirs amassed throughout his extensive voyages spanning from 1960 to 2023. With meticulous attention to detail, Angel ingeniously crafts a captivating map utilizing Scalable Vector Graphics (SVG) to elegantly position these cherished artifacts across various continents.

Angel's website creates a captivating color scheme primarily characterized by refreshing mint green, sophisticated navy blue, and pristine white. This thoughtful selection of hues creates a harmonious visual experience for visitors. To enhance user engagement, Angel implemented a toggle theme button, allowing users to dynamically alter the website's color palette. This delightful addition adds a playful and interactive dimension to the browsing experience, captivating users with a kaleidoscope of colors at their fingertips.

Within her website,the overview featuries a designed world map that highlights the precise locations where her father's invaluable collection. As users hover over the monochromatic images showcased on the map, a captivating transformation takes place. The images expands in size, revealing vibrant full-color while simultaneously providing contextual information about the corresponding location. This interactive and immersive experience grants visitors a deeper understanding of the collection's origins, bringing each cherished item to life with a vivid display of color and geographical significance.

Angel's website incorporates a practical feature in the form of a toggle all button, offering users the convenience of switching between two distinct scroll navigations. The first navigation option employs a horizontal scrollbar, enabling users to effortlessly navigate through content from left to right, providing a seamless browsing experience. The second option presents a vertical scrollbar, allowing users to scroll up and down to explore content at their own pace. This thoughtful inclusion ensures flexibility and accommodates diverse user preferences, enhancing accessibility and optimizing the overall user experience on Angel's website.
